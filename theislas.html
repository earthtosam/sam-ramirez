The Islas Marietas Restaurant

A mexican seafood restaurant in downtown Salinas. In this project I also helped in re-adjusting their restaurant logo and doing on-site photography of their restaurant and dishes.

Objective

Design a webpage that fit the style of the restaurant, while also appealing to a wider audience. Their current clientele was mainly Spanish-speaking, so a toggle between both English and Spanish was essential.

Answer

Represent the menu options by category. This allows clients to go straight to their desired menu items, and makes for a much faster UI experience.

What I did

Established a clear menu page with category buttons for a quicker experience. Also made menu items mobile friendly to expand on the focus of the dishes. The final webpage integrated all these answers to generate more views online to expand the restaurant's clientele.
