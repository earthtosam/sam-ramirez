Saul G. Photography

A local photographer who specializes in aviation photography. I wanted to create a webpage that matched his photography, and going with the color and typography that demonstrates his personality.

Objective

To create a webpage that matched his photography, and going with the color and typography that demonstrates his personality.

Answer

Layout work with titles and dates to get a better understanding of the photography timeline. At the same time, include writing that's more personality-driven.

What I did

Have the recent airshow events on the homepage. This would show variety in his work, while also showing a large bady of work. In addition, have the work be categorized by formation, with a date and title in each piece.
