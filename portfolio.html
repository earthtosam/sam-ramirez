SeeFood Website

I worked with a startup that aims to help local restaurant owners by having a platform to display their food lineup. My role as Lead Web Developer was to work on the development of the web platform. I worked with Firebase to deal with the data of all restaurants. Along with that, I also used React as a framework for the site. This allowed me to have a snappier web app and utilize component structures for each restaurant.

The Islas Marietas Restaurant

The Islas Marietas is a Mexican seefood restaurant located in downtown Salinas. One of the main issues that the owner was dealing with was the inconsistent menu that was posted online with platforms like Grubhub and Doordash. There would be outdated menu items and order would get unfilled. This affected their business, and needed a resolution. So with the website that I developed with them, we were able to provide clear and accurate info for their entire menu. The site also helped in making the restaurant more recognizable in local search, and bringing in more customers. Without dealing with outdated information.

Mama Lupitas Restaurant

Mama Lupitas was faced with a crucial problem. In the biginning of the worldwide pandemic, Mama Lupitas had to adapt to the regulations of keeping up a restaurant that met all the local health standards. But this meant that they couldn't pass out their physical menus. But they didn't have their menu up in the restaurant wall either. So they temporarily bought a cardboad poster and wrote their entire menu on it and placed it on an empty table. They needed a solution ASAP. So I developed a website that did just that, but also put more emphasis on what they valued the most; Tradition. So I kept the asthetics fairly familiar to the consumer, while also modernizing the platform without compromise.
