itssjessc Art

A local on-air personality at iHeart Radio who creates art pieces. I worked with her to make a webpage to showcase her work, by making it minimalist and drawing inspiration from a blank canvas.

Objective

To create a portfolio webpage that would showcase work from the artist, as well as a personal page that would explain a bit about herself.

Answer

Have a blank canvas for the webpage to emphasize the artwork. This would include having a page specifically for the portfolio, and categorizing each piece.

What I did

Included artwork throughout each page to reinforce the portfolio theme. This would also highlight each art piece and give the webpage a personality. Each page would have quick links to social media pages and have a designated contact form.
