About Me

I'm a web & graphic designer based in Salinas CA. I’ve always had an itch for design, wherever I looked. And as soon as I stepped into the world of web development, I found myself hooked from the beginnning. I have a passion for web design and love working on projects that revolve around design. I’ve been freelancing with local projects, but hope to work with a team to contribute my passion and dedication into the role.

What I do

I help people and businesses bring their work and passion into the digital space. I work with them to display their personality into the project, by focusing on design. Typography and UI/UX design is my favorite part in development. This helps deliver a better and memorable user experience that benefits everyone involved.
