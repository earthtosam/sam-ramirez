Creating a digital restaurant hub.


Islas Marietas came with me with the biggest problem they were facing when dealing with delivery apps: Incorrect menu information. The information found in a lot of these apps created confusion for clients, and also created a hassle for management to keep up with. To solve this, I made a website that would not only display the correct menu information, but also drive traffic to the site. This would rank up the site with the enhanced SEO I worked on, and strengthen the online reliability of the restaurant.
