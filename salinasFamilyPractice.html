Salinas Family Practice

Previously working in the clinic, I was given the opportunity to redesign their webpage. Webpage visits are important for the clinic. Not only to include accurate information, but to have contact information that could be critical for someone in need.

Objective

Focus heavily in UI & UX by making it more accesible to anyone who visted the webpage to contact the clinic. This makes it easier for anyone to get in touch.

Answer

Include accessible contact buttons on each page to speak with the staff or nurse. This allows patients to easily contact the clinic after reviewing information.

What I did

Emphasized on quick contact bars on each page. This allows for quick contact, and a simple UI experience. Other pages such as patient resources, services, and staff information get dedicated "cards" with social links. Contact information is the main focus, and this is highlighted throughout the website.
